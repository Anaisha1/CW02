Photography is the art and practice of capturing light to create images that tell stories, preserve memories, and express creativity. Through photography, people can document important moments and share unique perspectives of the world around them.

Modern photography combines technical knowledge with artistic vision. Photographers use cameras, lighting, composition, and editing techniques to create compelling images that communicate emotions and ideas.

Photography can be used in many fields, including journalism, advertising, fashion, wildlife, travel, and portraiture. Each style requires different skills and approaches, making photography a diverse and exciting creative discipline.
